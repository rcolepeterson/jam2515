About

Search, Share and Vote on videos with your friends in real time.

1. Search for a video.
2. Add it to the playlist.
3. Vote off videos that you dislike.
4. Enjoy.

 

This is a proof of concept application built on the Meteor / Node.js platform. Everything you see on the screen is happening in real time, meaning everyone logged in is seeing the same thing.
